GONTREL  ·  STORYBOARD
She's Still Scrolling?
Turtle & Hare concept
CAST: 4 FEMALE TALENT
RUNTIME: 35 SEC MAX
LOCATIONS: OFFICE · SALON · RESTAURANT
1OFFICE (INT.) — ESTABLISHING & CHAOTIC 0:00 – 0:08

Quick close-ups on Girl A and Girl B at their workstations, wrapping up the day — brief, ordinary energy, no lingering. It then tips fast into chaos: both scrambling across two devices, phone and laptop. 3D screen projections show rapid scrolling on each. Talking over each other, not in sync.

GIRL B

"This one's too far."

GIRL A

"Wait, what about this—"

2NAIL SALON (INT.) — CALM 0:08 – 0:13

Camera cuts to nail salon scene. Girl C relaxed, getting nails done by Girl D (whose face is not shown). Small 3D phone-screen projection — one confident scroll on Gontrel, booked in a couple of taps. Sets phone down, continues to do her nails at ease.

3OFFICE (INT.) — RUSHED 0:13 – 0:19

Camera cuts back to the office. Papers are flying all around the room. Girl A lands on a place, scrambling to pull up Google Maps for the route. Bags grabbed fast, frantic energy.

GIRL A

"We have to go now!"

GIRL B

"Wait, is it even the right location?"

GIRL A

"Just go, go, go!"

4TRANSITION TO RESTAURANT 0:19 – 0:23

Quick cut back to the nail salon — Girl C's chair is empty, she already left. Girl D cleaning up, unbothered. Straight cut to exterior restaurant: Girl A and Girl B finally arrive together, flustered, catching their breath.

GIRL B

"Wait—"

GIRL A

"Is that?"

5INTERIOR RESTAURANT 0:23 – 0:35

Girl A and Girl B arrive outside the restaurant and spot Girl C already seated through the window/door. A waiter approaches Girl C's table. Camera cuts to a wider, further-away shot showing Girl C and the waiter mid-conversation as Girl A and Girl B walk up to join them. Their dialogue plays in the background as they approach. Closing shot: phone on the table, Gontrel app visible, everyone softly out of focus behind it.

WAITER

"How did you find out about us?"

GIRL C

(background, as the others approach)

"Gontrel."

GIRL A

"Should have just used that."

GIRL C (V.O.)

(voice over)

"Stop scrolling. Start eating."

Gontrel

0:33–0:35

Production note: Turtle & hare concept — Gontrel (calm/direct) vs. multi-app chaos (TikTok, Google Maps, OpenTable). Girl D's face is not shown on camera. Slogan "Stop scrolling. Start eating." is delivered as a voice over (V.O.) closing line, rather than shown as an on-screen tagline card. Total scripted runtime: 35 seconds, hitting the agreed max.
